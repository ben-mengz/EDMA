## Agents Involved
- sem_agent

## Steps
1. **Turn on the inLens detector**: Firstly turn on the inLens detector(make it run) via function.
2. **Confirm ROI**: Confirm the Region of Interest (ROI) for the scan.
3. **Set Defocus**: Set the beam defocus to the target value.
4. **Acquire Data**: Execute the 4D-STEM acquisition sequence.
